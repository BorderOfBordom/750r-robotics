SBHS Team 750R

Who Are We?

Team 750R is a VEX EDR team from South Brunswick High School in New Jersey. The team is comprised of four sophomores, four juniors, and one senior. We have been participating in VEX competitions in the central New Jersey area since early 2018.

What is VEX?

VEX Robotics was created as a solution to the growing need for people in STEM fields of work. By educating children about engineering principles and the design process, VEX hopes to prepare students for a job in science, technology, and mathematics. To this end, VEX uses games and competitions to bring fun to learning, with this year's competition being Turning Point.

This Year's Game

Turning Point is a game in which two alliances of two teams each compete to score as many points as possible by flipping caps and toggling flags. The game features an autonomous section, during which the robot acts without driver input, and a driver control section, which tests the skills of the alliances' drive teams.
